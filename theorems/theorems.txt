th.prop.ax1
impl a. (impl b. a.)
First axiom of propositional logic

th.prop.ax2
impl (impl a. (impl b. c.)) (impl (impl a. b.) (impl a. c.))
Second axiom of propositional logic

th.prop.ax3
impl (impl (pnot a.) (pnot b.)) (impl b. a.)
Third axiom of propositional logic

th.prop.impl.refl
impl a. a.
Implication is reflexive

th.prop.impl.tran
impl (impl a. b.) (impl (impl b. c.) (impl a. c.))
Implication is transitive

th.prop.reord
impl (impl a. (impl b. c.)) (impl b. (impl a. c.))
First two antecedents can be swapped

th.prop.ax2.conv
impl (impl (impl a. b.) (impl a. c.)) (impl a. (impl b. c.))
Converse of the second axiom of propositional logic

th.prop.ant.intr
impl (impl a. b.) (impl (impl c. a.) (impl c. b.))
Any antecedent can be added to both implication operands

th.prop.ant.intr.ext
impl (impl a. (impl b. c.)) (impl (impl d. a.) (impl (impl d. b.) (impl d. c.)))
Extension of "th.prop.ant.intr"

th.prop.dneg.intr
impl a. (pnot (pnot a.))
Double negation introduction

th.prop.dneg.elim
impl (pnot (pnot a.)) a.
Double negation elimination

th.prop.ax3.conv
impl (impl a. b.) (impl (pnot b.) (pnot a.))
Converse of the third axiom of propositional logic
